It would also be helpful to conduct an activity-based costing model, in which the different sources of cash inflows and outflows are listed in detail for reference, and thus the budgeting process for the next auditing term will be smoothier. For example, if we can grab a sense on how much the SG&A (assuming 80% of SG&A is personnel wages) constitutes the overall operating expenses, by budgeting for SG&A for the next year we can also get an estimation for the overall financial pattern.

Another thing to note is that, in my projection, even if the discount rate is 0%, the NPV is -$31,000. Hypothetically, the discount rate could be up to 20% in the indie game industry due to a high volatility in market demand (IndieNova, 2021.) This number could be justified since there is hardly profiting independent game company can break even in the first five years, given that magnates are competing hard in promoting games of their own. However, as we stated in the exit strategy part previously, the goal is not only to profit but also to stand out of hundreds of players to be seen by potential institutional buyers. Negative NPV is not necessarily a negative signal in the case for indie game companies.

The valuations of indie game companies vary significantly due to the diversity of their products, capital requirements, and product development cycles. To quantitatively value an indie game company, one simplified and quick method is to estimate the future market potential and the market share the company can acquire. By multiplying the market potential by the market share, we can obtain a rough estimate of the company's value.
